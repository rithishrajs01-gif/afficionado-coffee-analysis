# Executive Summary
## Afficionado Coffee Roasters — Sales Trend & Time-Based Performance Analysis

**Date:** July 2025 | **Prepared For:** Stakeholders & Management  
**Period Analyzed:** January – July 2025 | **Total Transactions:** 149,116

---

## Business Overview

Afficionado Coffee Roasters operates 3 specialty coffee stores in New York City (Lower Manhattan, Hell's Kitchen, Astoria), generating **$698,812 in total revenue** across 149,116 transactions in the first half of 2025.

---

## Top 5 Findings

**1. Morning is everything.**  
The 7–11 AM window generates **54% of all daily revenue**. The single peak hour — 10:00 AM — alone accounts for $88,673 in revenue and 18,545 transactions. This is the most operationally critical window of the day.

**2. Every day of the week performs equally.**  
Daily revenue varies by only **1.3%** across all 7 days ($99,287 to $100,597). There is no meaningful "slow day" — Afficionado benefits from a loyal, routine-driven customer base.

**3. All three stores follow the same pattern.**  
Hell's Kitchen, Astoria, and Lower Manhattan each contribute approximately one-third of total revenue (33.8% / 33.2% / 32.9%) and share identical hourly demand curves. No location is underperforming.

**4. Late evenings are not profitable.**  
After 7:00 PM, transaction volume collapses. Hour 8 PM records only 603 transactions — **97% below peak**. Remaining open beyond 8 PM is not financially justified.

**5. Coffee and Tea drive the business.**  
Together, Coffee ($269,952) and Tea ($196,406) account for **66.7% of all revenue**. These categories are the business-critical product lines.

---

## Recommended Actions

| Priority | Action | Expected Benefit |
|----------|--------|-----------------|
| HIGH | Maximum staffing from 7–11 AM daily | Capture full morning rush revenue |
| HIGH | Close all stores by 8:00 PM | Reduce evening operating costs |
| MEDIUM | Maintain stable afternoon team (12–5 PM) | Serve consistent secondary demand |
| MEDIUM | Protect coffee & tea quality and supply chain | Defend 66.7% revenue base |
| LOW | Add calendar date to transaction records | Enable seasonal trend analysis |

## Conclusion

Afficionado Coffee Roasters is a financially healthy, operationally consistent business. The data reveals no crisis — but it does reveal a clear optimization opportunity: **align staffing costs with actual demand curves**. Investing heavily in the 7–11 AM window and pulling back after 7 PM could significantly improve profitability without impacting customer experience.
